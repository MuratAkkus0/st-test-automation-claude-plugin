## Notes for future runs

The transient `/api/product/redirectWithCheck` 404 on the first attempt — after portal consent was confirmed — is worth tracking. If it reproduces on a fourth run, it likely indicates a rate-limit or per-visitor-id state on the moebel.de redirect endpoint when the same partner is hit multiple times in quick succession. Workaround: retry the same redirect URL once after a few seconds. If the second attempt also 404s, that is no longer transient and warrants investigation.
