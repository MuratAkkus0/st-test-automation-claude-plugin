## Root Cause Analysis

There is no failure to attribute on the Base side ‚Äî the Base Tag is correctly configured in GTM, fires on page load with consent active, and writes the moeclid from the URL into `MOEBEL_CLICKOUT_ID` in localStorage on the first page load after consent acceptance. The integration is ready on the partner side; what is missing is the portal-side product syndication that would let real users land on Emob product pages with a moeclid attached.

1. **Push Emob's products live on meubelo.nl** (portal-side action). Once products are syndicated and visible on `/shops/emob`, the standard end-to-end test can be re-run with a product-tile click ‚Üí product page ‚Üí add to cart ‚Üí order submission ‚Üí Conversion Tag verification.
2. **Re-run the test with order submission enabled** once products are live, to verify:
   - The Conversion Tag fires on the order-success page
   - The PARTNER_KEY matches what ACM has on file for Emob NL
   - The `total` / `shipping` / `items` payload is correct

3. **Reference documentation** for any follow-up troubleshooting:
